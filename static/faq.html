Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sassy Chic and how does it work?
Sassy Chic is a personalized fashion platform designed to help you discover styles that suit your face shape, features, and personality. By combining visual inputs and smart recommendations, the platform suggests outfits, colors, and aesthetics that enhance your natural look.
Is the styling recommendation truly personalized?
Yes. The system focuses on individuality — your facial structure, tone, and preferences are considered to generate tailored suggestions instead of generic fashion advice. The goal is to help you build a style identity that feels authentic to you.
Do I need any prior fashion knowledge?
Not at all. The platform is designed for everyone — whether you're a beginner exploring your style or someone experienced looking to refine it. The interface is intuitive and guides you step-by-step.
Is my data and image safe?
User privacy is treated with high importance. Images are processed securely and are not stored unnecessarily. The focus is on delivering results while maintaining trust and safety.
Can I use this for daily styling inspiration?
Absolutely. Sassy Chic can be used not just for one-time recommendations but as an ongoing styling assistant — helping you explore new looks, trends, and combinations that align with your personal aesthetic.
